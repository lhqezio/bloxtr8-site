# To bring you support and help every step of the way

## If you’re a human, you’re a creative

It’s no secret that the economy isn’t what it was used to be, endless lay-offs, just in the first quarter of 2026, when I’m writing this, Dell laid off 10% of its workforce-about 11,000 jobs. Everyone is fighting to get by, record number of unemployment, economy is getting worse and worse. Most people when face the uncertainty of the future either completely give up and mindlessly consume medias or they fall for degenerate get-rich-quick schemes, especially gambling in its modern forms: day trading on stock market, prediction market, sport betting. More swings. More bets. More chances that one big win might change their financial life forever, so that they may escape the permanent underclass. We, at Bloxtr8, reject that message, we reject that your whole future lies on chance, on a lucky break, It tells a generation they're prisoners, trapped in a broken economy. Then it sells them a lottery ticket and calls it freedom. 

So what’s the alternative? BUILD. build something, build anything, edit a video, create content, draw a painting, write a song, start a bussiness even; be a creator, not a consumer, Tyler the Creator wouldnt have the same aura if he’s called Tyler the Consumer now would he. The difference between creating and gambling is that you’re building something real, something that can keep growing, instead of burning money on a game designed to take it from you. And in our case, we want to help you to the best of our ability to create value, something of your own in the Roblox ecosystem. We all heard the stories of Jandel’s Grow a garden, a dev with a small team and big vision, grew it to the biggest game on the roblox platform, or Newfissy and Bethink’s Adopt me which made 60 Milions USD/year at one point (allegedly). We want to help you to join the club of creators who successfully make a living doing what they love and became an inspiration for many more in the community. It doesn’t matter what you do, we’re here to help, we help you connecting with others by foster a community of likeminded creators, we provide you the tools and the means to create and get compensate fairly. If you build it then you own it, you’re free to express however way you see fit, make it you because you’re the best at it.

Remember, delusional optimism and relentless effort are the only way out. And I believe in you, I believe you could make it. You got it twin, you literally have nothing to lose, flopping in creating is better than slaying in consuming. So let’s build, we got you twin.

-- Minh, Bloxtr8's COO --
